Privacy Policy for Kids Shoe Sizer

Last updated: February 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Kids Shoe Sizer (com.bojan.kidsshoesize) handles data when you use the app.

Contact: bob.marin9613@gmail.com

1. Data We Process

A. Data You Enter (stored on your device)

  • Child profile details (for example: name, birthday, shoe sizes)
  • Growth records (for example: dates, foot length, notes)
  • App settings (theme, language, measurement unit, notifications, ad-removal status)

This local data is saved on your device and is not sent to our own servers.

B. Data Processed by Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob (ads)
  • Google Play Billing (in-app purchase: remove ads)
  • Google Play Services / Android platform services (distribution, integrity, diagnostics)

These services may process technical/device data such as device identifiers, IP address, app interactions, purchase data, and diagnostics according to their own policies.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

2. How Data Is Used

  • Provide shoe size conversion and growth tracking features
  • Save your preferences and app state
  • Show ads (unless ads are removed via purchase)
  • Process and restore purchases
  • Schedule local reminders (if you enable notifications)

3. Notifications and Permissions

The app may request these permissions:

  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: to send reminder notifications
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED: to restore reminders after reboot
  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM: to schedule reminder timing
  • REQUEST_IGNORE_BATTERY_OPTIMIZATIONS: optional, for reminder reliability
  • INTERNET: for ads, billing, and store-related services
  • com.android.vending.BILLING: for in-app purchases

You can deny or revoke permissions in your device settings.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not operate our own backend to collect profile data. Data may still be processed by third-party SDKs/services listed above.

5. Data Retention

  • Local app data remains on your device until deleted, app storage is cleared, or app is uninstalled.
  • Third-party providers retain data according to their own policies.

6. Children's Privacy

Kids Shoe Sizer is intended for parents/caregivers. The app does not require account creation and we do not knowingly collect children's personal data on our own servers.

7. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding personal data processed by third parties (for example access, deletion, objection, or restriction rights). For third-party processing requests, please review and contact the relevant provider directly.

For app-related privacy questions, contact: bob.marin9613@gmail.com

8. Security

We use reasonable measures available in the app and platform, but no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by changing the "Last updated" date.
